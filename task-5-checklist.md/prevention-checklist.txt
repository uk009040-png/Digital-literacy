Stay Safe from Cybercrime – Checklist

1.Do not share OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone
2.Avoid posting sensitive details (phone, address, ID) online
3.Use strong, unique passwords for every account
4.Install apps only from trusted sources (Play Store/App Store)
5.Keep your phone and software updated
6.Use screen lock (PIN, fingerprint, or face lock)
7.Install antivirus/security apps
8.Use trusted apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm
9.Never enter UPI PIN to receive money
10.Always check payment requests before approving
11.Avoid scanning unknown QR codes
12.Do not open unknown attachments
13.Verify sender before replying
14.Watch for spelling errors or fake offers
15.Never share confidential info via email
