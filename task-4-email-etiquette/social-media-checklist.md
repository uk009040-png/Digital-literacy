# Social Media Do's and Don'ts
## For College Students

## ✅ Do's

1. Keep your profiles clean and professional
2. Think before you post — ask yourself if your 
   professor or future employer would approve
3. Use social media to showcase your skills 
   and achievements
4. Follow industry experts and professionals 
   in your field
5. Keep your privacy settings updated and 
   reviewed regularly

## ❌ Don'ts

1. Never share your personal details like phone 
   number or address publicly
2. Never post offensive, racist, or inappropriate 
   content
3. Never share confidential information about 
   your college or classmates
4. Never engage in cyberbullying or online 
   arguments
5. Never share your passwords or OTPs with anyone
